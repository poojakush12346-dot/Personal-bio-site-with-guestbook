About Me

Hello! I'm Pooja Kushwaha, a passionate learner and aspiring Full Stack Developer who enjoys creating modern web applications and solving real-world problems through technology. I love exploring modern tools, diving into code, and building practical applications that bridge creativity and functionality.
I grew up tinkering with computers — disassembling them, putting them back together, and eventually writing my first lines of HTML at age 18. That curiosity never left me. I pursued Computer Science at AKTU University, where I discovered a deep love for elegant systems and the human side of technology. While graduating I joined a fast-growing fintech startup as a junior engineer, quickly taking ownership of the customer-facing dashboard. I learned how to balance speed and quality under real product pressure — and how much the smallest UX details matter to users. Today I work as a full-stack developer, splitting my time between product work and open-source contributions. I believe good software is kind: it respects the user's time, is honest about its limitations, and gets out of the way.
